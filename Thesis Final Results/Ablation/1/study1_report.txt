Analysis:
----------------------------------------
Best configuration:  alpha=0.9, beta=0.1  (avg_reward=-0.0151)
Chosen config:       alpha=0.9, beta=0.1  (avg_reward=-0.0151)

The sweep shows a clear trend along the normalized pairs:
higher alpha (speed weight) and correspondingly lower beta
(quality penalty) produce higher rewards and speedups,
confirming alpha=0.9, beta=0.1 as the optimal trade-off point.
The normalization constraint (alpha+beta=1.0) ensures the reward
components are properly balanced, avoiding the scaling issue where
unnormalized weights (e.g. alpha=0.8, beta=0.1 = 0.9 total)
produce artificially inflated or deflated reward magnitudes.
